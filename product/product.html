The Grounding Ritual

When the mind is racing and the body feels depleted, Ashwagandha acts as a powerful grounding force. Known as "Indian Ginseng," it has been used for millennia to build vitality and resistance to stress. Our blend balances the earthy bitterness of Ashwagandha with the sweet warmth of cinnamon and the floral uplift of rose petals.

Pure Botanical Ingredients

  • Organic Ashwagandha Root: The ultimate adaptogen for stress relief.
  • Organic Tulsi (Holy Basil): Elevates the spirit and supports immunity.
  • Organic Cinnamon Bark: Aids digestion and circulation.
  • Organic Cardamom & Rose Petals: Cools and harmonizes the blend.

Preparation Ritual

Steep 1 heaping teaspoon of loose leaf tea in 8oz of freshly boiled water (212°F). Cover and let steep for 7-10 minutes. For a traditional Ayurvedic nighttime remedy, simmer gently in warm milk (or oat milk) with a touch of ghee and raw honey.
